### **Checkers Core Rules**

* **Goal**: Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking them from moving.
* **Setup**: 12 pieces per player on a 64-square board (32 black, 32 red).
* **Movement**:
    * Black moves first.
    * Pieces move diagonally forward one square on black tiles.
* **Jumping (Mandatory)**:
    * If a jump is possible, you **must** take it.
    * Multiple jumps are allowed in a single turn.
    * Captured pieces are removed from the board.
* **Kings**:
    * Pieces reaching the "King Me" row become Kings.
    * Kings move and jump both forward and backward.
